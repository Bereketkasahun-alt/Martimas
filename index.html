Our Story

About Martimas

Martimas is a luxury Ethiopian fashion and lifestyle brand born from a deep love of our cultural heritage and an uncompromising pursuit of quality. From the highlands of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, we bring handcrafted clothing, premium leather bags, statement jewellery, and artisan home decor to the world stage.

Each piece is a testament to the skill of Ethiopian artisans — weavers, leather workers, goldsmiths, and decorators who pour generations of knowledge into every product we create.

  • ✦ Authentic Ethiopian Craftsmanship
  • ✦ Uncompromising Quality Standards
  • ✦ Modern Luxury Aesthetic
  • ✦ Sustainable & Ethical Production
